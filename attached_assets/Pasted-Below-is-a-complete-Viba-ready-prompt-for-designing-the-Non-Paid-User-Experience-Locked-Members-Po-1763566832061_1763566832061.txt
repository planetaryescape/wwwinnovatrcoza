✅ VIBA DESIGN PROMPT — NON-PAID USER EXPERIENCE + LOCKED PORTAL SYSTEM

PROJECT: Build a seamless sign-up and restricted-access experience for non-paid users, allowing them to:
• Sign up for bi-weekly mailers (Pulse Insights)
• Create a free portal account
• Access a limited / preview version of the Members Portal
• See locked sections that become available once a membership is purchased
• Receive upsell prompts that encourage upgrading

This experience should feel friendly, modern, aspirational — encouraging non-members to see the value of upgrading.

⸻

🧭 KEY REQUIREMENTS

Non-paid users must have access to:
	•	Account creation/login
	•	Members Portal (restricted version)
	•	Dashboard preview with limited tiles
	•	A small set of free insights (teaser content only)
	•	Ability to:
• subscribe to bi-weekly mailers
• read select free reports
• start a Test24 PAYG purchase (Basic or Pro)
• view available memberships

⸻

Non-paid users must NOT have access to:

(All these sections must appear locked, blurred, or greyed out.)
	•	Full Trend Library
	•	Unlimited Trend Reports
	•	Member-only deals & discounts
	•	Personalised recommendation engine
	•	Full Past Research Dashboard
	•	Credit balance + top-up system
	•	Private Dashboards
	•	Dedicated insights expert
	•	Member-only pricing (Basic = R5k/idea, Pro = R45k/study)

These features must show upgrade prompts, tooltips, and “Unlock with Membership” buttons.

⸻

🎨 OVERALL VIBE
	•	Clean, modern SaaS design
	•	Clear separation between Free and Member-Only
	•	Friendly upgrade nudges (“You’re almost there…”)
	•	Portal should feel valuable even in free mode
	•	Maintain Innovatr brand consistency, neon accent + minimal layout

⸻

🏠 HOMEPAGE ADDITION — MAILER SIGN-UP SECTION

Add a dedicated section near the bottom of the homepage:

Section Title:

Stay Ahead of the Shift

Subline:

Get free bi-weekly insights, market shifts, launches and trend analysis delivered to your inbox.

CTA Field:

Email input + button: “Subscribe to Pulse Insights”

Additional note (small text):

“Get early access to select trends and reports. Full library available with an Innovatr Membership.”

⸻

🧭 NON-PAID USER PORTAL STRUCTURE

The Members Portal should detect user type (FREE vs MEMBER) and adjust automatically.

⸻

1. Free User Dashboard (Preview Mode)

A. Welcome Panel

“Welcome, [Name]. You’re exploring the Innovatr Portal.”
Badge: Free Account

B. Quick Actions Row
	•	Subscribe to Pulse Insights (active)
	•	Run Test24 Basic (R10k) (active)
	•	Browse Membership Plans (active)
	•	View Free Reports (active)

C. Locked Value Cards (Greyed / Blurred):
	•	“Full Trend Library — Locked”
	•	“Member Deals — Locked”
	•	“Private Dashboard — Locked”
	•	“Personalised Recommendations — Locked”

Each with CTA: Unlock with Membership

D. Free Insights Teaser Section

Show 2–3 free trend snapshots with blurred previews of additional reports.
CTA: Unlock Full Library

⸻

2. Trend & Insights Library (Restricted)
	•	Show only one free sample report per category.
	•	All other reports greyed out with:
“Members Only — Unlock to Download”
	•	Keep filter/search active but results locked for non-members.

⸻

3. Launch a Brief (Active for Non-Paid)

Non-members can launch Test24 using PAYG pricing:
	•	Basic: R10k per idea
	•	Pro: R50k per x100 study

Membership discount pricing should not show until upgraded.

After brief submission → portal prompts upgrade for:
“Unlock private dashboards + research discounts with Membership.”

⸻

4. Past Research Dashboard (Locked)

Non-paid users see:
	•	“You have no research history”
	•	Greyed out sample cards labelled “Member Feature”
	•	CTA: Unlock Past Results with Membership

⸻

5. Credits & Billing (Locked)

Non-paid users see:
	•	Zero credits
	•	Greyed out credit meter
	•	Message: “Credits are exclusive to Innovatr Members.”
	•	CTA: Become a Member to Unlock Discounts + Credits

⸻

6. Member Deals (Locked)

Promo tiles are visible but blurred.
Click → modal explaining:
“Exclusive to Innovatr Members. Join to unlock up to 40% savings.”

⸻

🔐 HOW LOCKING BEHAVES
	•	Grey overlay
	•	Small lock icon corner label
	•	Hovering displays tooltip: “Unlock with Membership”
	•	CTA always available to redirect to Membership Plans

⸻

🎯 UPGRADE MECHANICS

Whenever a non-paid user interacts with a locked feature, trigger a modal:

Modal Title:
“Unlock Full Innovatr Access”

Text:
Get unlimited trends, member pricing, private dashboards, research credits, and exclusive monthly deals.

CTAs:
	•	See Membership Plans
	•	Continue Browsing

⸻

📱 RESPONSIVENESS

Experience must remain clean and functional on mobile, with collapsible sections.

⸻

✨ FINAL GOAL

Create a flawless system where:
	•	Free users feel welcome
	•	Paid members feel powerful
	•	Locked features create desire to upgrade
	•	Bi-weekly mailer sign-up is frictionless
	•	Membership becomes the obvious next step
