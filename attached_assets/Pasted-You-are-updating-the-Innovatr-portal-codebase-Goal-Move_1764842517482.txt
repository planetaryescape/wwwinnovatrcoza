6. Credits: buy, store, and use

We need credits to be real, per account, not just a visual.

6.1 Buying credits

When someone buys credits:

If logged in:

Create an Order tied to their userId + companyId.

Update a CompanyCredits table or append a row to a CreditLedger.

If not logged in (e.g. marketing checkout on the site):

Encourage sign-in / sign-up before purchase where possible.

At minimum, ensure the purchase is later linkable to a user/company by email.

6.2 Recommended: credit ledger

Instead of only storing raw balances, maintain a CreditLedger:

id

companyId

type ('purchase' | 'use' | 'adjustment')

studyType ('basic' | 'pro' | etc.)

quantity (positive for purchase, negative for use)

orderId (optional link)

briefId (for usage entries)

createdAt

Then compute balances as:

basicCredits = sum(quantity where type in purchase/adjustment for basic)
proCredits   = sum(quantity where type in purchase/adjustment for pro)
...


This makes auditing and debugging much easier.

6.3 Using credits on brief submission

When a logged-in user submits a brief and selects “Use my credits”:

Check their company’s relevant credit balance.

If sufficient:

Deduct by inserting a negative entry in CreditLedger linked to that briefId.

Mark the brief’s payment method as credits.

If insufficient:

Show a clear message:
“You do not have enough credits for this study. Please top up or choose another payment option.”

Offer a Top up credits route.

Whenever the portal shows “Available credits”, use the ledger/balance, not a hard-coded number.
