Innovatr Inside: Storyteller
If you have ever waited for a research report that took so long you forgot what the study was even about, Storyteller is the reason that era is finally ending. Storyteller is the narrative engine built inside Upsiide that turns finished studies into structured stories as soon as fieldwork closes. It does the heavy lifting so your team can focus on decisions, not deck building.
Storyteller analyses every part of a completed study at once and produces a clean, readable and strategically sound narrative. It is not a page of fluffy highlights or a pile of disconnected charts. It is a report that already feels like someone understood the brief, spotted the important patterns and shaped a flow that leadership will actually follow.
Because Innovatr holds the licence for South Africa and Southern Africa, Storyteller is a capability that very few insight or innovation teams in the region have access to. It is trained on thousands of real research projects, strategic frameworks and the way strong insights are written. This means the starting point is already meaningful. It behaves like a junior consultant who arrives with a first draft that is surprisingly sharp.
Where Storyteller fits in your business life cycle
In Innovatr language, Storyteller sits mainly in How to win and What is working.
• In How to win, it turns test results into business cases, launch stories and clear recommendations.
• In What is working, it helps teams track shifts over time and translate tracking data into simple internal stories.
So Storyteller is not just a reporting feature. It is one of the ways we help you move from data to decision across your business.
What Storyteller is built to do
These are the moments where Storyteller shines:
• It turns messy data into smart, structured insight blocks with the right charts and plain language explanations.
• It builds a narrative that lands with teams beyond research. If your CFO or creative director reads it, they will follow it.
• It flags meaningful differences and strengths and it does that with statistical confidence rather than intuition.
• It can create focused versions of the story for specific audiences or segments without starting again from zero.
This is the kind of reporting that keeps everyone aligned without adding weeks of manual work.
What this feels like when you work with us
A typical Innovatr workflow is simple.
We launch your study to our South African panel of around eight hundred thousand people who represent the demographic profile of the country. If you are working across markets, we extend fieldwork into Zimbabwe, Zambia, Namibia, Rwanda, DRC or Tunisia, and use our recruitment team for audiences panels cannot reach.
Once fieldwork finishes, Storyteller produces the first draft of the report within minutes. The Innovatr team then reviews the system generated insights, sharpens the message, adds local category context and provides commercial clarity. The result is a narrative that is quick but considered. It lands with senior teams because it has been shaped with real experience.
This is also why Test24 feels fast without feeling lightweight. By automating the mechanical work inside Upsiide we free the team to focus on the thinking. Member pricing from about R15 000 for Basic and about R45 000 for Pro becomes possible because we are not spending days building slides. You are paying for interpretation, judgement and relevance, not formatting.
When you should use Storyteller
Storyteller is ideal when you want to reduce the time between insight and action. Use it when you need clarity within a short window, when you are testing multiple ideas or formats, when you have several audiences that each need a tailored view or when you want something better than a long document no one will finish.
Storyteller creates reports that travel easily inside organisations. They are clean, confident and simple to follow. For teams that work at the pace of modern marketing, this is a major advantage.
