Please read everything carefully and then implement the changes step by step.

Does not require a company

Shows clearly as “Independent” in the Admin → Members table

Uses the same tier system (Free, Starter, Growth, Scale)

Can launch briefs and pay once-off

Does not appear as a Company under Admin → Companies

Ignore Stripe completely. We are not using Stripe in this project.
