✅ VIBA DESIGN PROMPT — INNOVATR MEMBERS PORTAL

PROJECT: Design a modern, intuitive, personalised Members Portal for Innovatr.
USERS: Innovation teams, corporate marketers, founders, agencies, and insights professionals who have purchased a membership (Entry, Gold, Platinum).

GOAL: Deliver a seamless, personalised experience that helps members:
• Discover relevant trends & insights
• Launch new briefs instantly
• Manage and top up research credits
• Access past research results
• Unlock exclusive member-only value
• Increase ongoing engagement

🎨 OVERALL PORTAL STYLE & UX

Premium SaaS dashboard look: bright white, charcoal, and neon highlight accents aligned to Innovatr brand.

Minimalist, distraction-free interface with large cards, spacious layout, clean typography.

Personalised welcome panel at the top: “Welcome back, [Name]” + membership tier badge (Entry / Gold / Platinum).

Sticky left-hand navigation with icons for each section.

Use micro-animations and progress indicators (e.g., credit usage bars).

Mobile-responsive.

Intuitive, very fast to navigate, “1-click access” to all core actions.

🧭 PORTAL STRUCTURE & PAGES

Design the portal with the following primary sections:

1. Home Dashboard (Personalised Overview)

Purpose: A personalised value snapshot.

Components to include:

A. Member Profile Capsule

Name

Company

Membership Tier (Entry / Gold / Platinum)

Benefits summary

Progress indicators (e.g., Basic ideas used / remaining)

B. “Your Quick Actions” Row (CTA Squares)

Launch New Test24 Brief

Download Latest Trend Reports

Buy More Research Credits

View Past Studies

Each CTA uses vibrant icon-based tiles.

C. Personalised Recommendations Feed

Auto-curated based on industry and past interactions:

Industry-specific trends

Competitor alerts

Suggested topics to test next

New monthly reports

Use a dynamic card carousel.

D. Credit Summary Widget

Real-time:

Credits remaining

Expiry (if applicable)

Suggested top-up bundles

One-click “Top Up” button

E. Member Deals Box

Monthly discounts

Early access offers

Bonus reports

Exclusive bundles for members

Clear CTA: Explore Deals

2. Trend & Insights Library

Goal: Give members access to local, industry-relevant intelligence tied to their profile.

Required Features:

Personalised filter: auto-show industry-related reports first

Search bar (topic, category, trend type, market)

Sort options: newest, most downloaded, recommended

Report Cards:

Title

Summary

Date

Download button

Tag system (Category: Beverage / Retail / Innovation / Youth / Design etc.)

Monthly new report indicator (“NEW” badge)

Bookmarking/favourite reports

Optional: “Based on your industry, we recommend:” row.

3. Launch a New Brief (Briefing Forms)

Goal: Allow members to launch new Test24 Basic or Pro studies instantly.

Design Requirements:

Two big cards: Test24 Basic or Test24 Pro

Simple guided form:

Select product type

Upload ideas (text, image, video)

Select target audience

Add competitor concepts (optional)

Add research questions (if Pro)

Confirm and launch

Auto-save drafts

Confirmation screen showing:

Estimated turnaround time (24hr)

Credit cost

Status tracker (“In Field → Processing → Ready”)

UX Tone:

Fast, lightweight, “three steps & done” feel.

4. Credits & Billing Section

Goal: Transparency and control over credit usage.

Features:

Current credit balance (Basic Credits + Pro Credits)

Animated progress bars (used / available)

Membership discounts automatically displayed

Purchase buttons:

Buy Basic Credit Packs

Buy Pro Credit Packs

Auto-renew toggle (optional)

Billing history table

VAT invoices downloadable

Payment method management

UX:

Clean, easy, highly visual.

5. Past Research Dashboard

Goal: Give members instant access to their full testing history.

Features:

List and grid view options

Filters: date, brand, category, product type, score, Test24 Basic vs Pro

Each Study Card includes:

Study title

Date

Performance highlights (top-line metric snapshot)

“Open Dashboard” button

“Download Full Report” button

For Pro studies:

AI Qual video snippet previews

“View VOC Library” button

Detail Page:

A beautiful, clean results page displaying:

Idea ranking

Market simulator

Emotional/attitudinal diagnostics

Quadrant chart

Theme summaries

Verbatims

Export options (PDF, PPTX)

6. Member-Only Deals Area

Goal: Encourage upsell & ongoing engagement.

Features:

Monthly discount cards (e.g., “Buy 3 ideas, get 1 free”)

Bundle upgrades (Gold → Platinum)

Limited-time offers

Exclusive trend reports

Design as promotional tiles with strong visual identity.

7. Settings / Profile

Update profile

Change industry preference (affects trend recommendations)

Notification preferences

Team access (add/remove users)

Integrations (optional future)

🧩 CROSS-FUNCTIONAL FEATURES
1. Personalisation Engine

Every page should adapt based on:

Member tier

Industry

Past tests

Download behaviour

Portal activity

2. Engagement Nudges

“You have unused credits”

“A new trend report is available in your industry”

“Your brief draft is waiting to be completed”

“3 ideas from competitors are trending in your market”

3. Gamified Value Meter (Optional)

Shows how much value the member has unlocked this year.

📱 VISUAL STYLE GUIDELINES

Crisp SaaS aesthetic

High contrast with neon accent highlights

Rounded corner tiles

Micro shadows for depth

Simple, generous spacing

Modern iconography

No clutter — always 1 primary action per screen

🎯 OVERALL FEEL

The portal should feel like a smart research command centre — personalised, powerful, and effortless.
Fast decisions. Smart insights. Total control.
